Frequently Asked Questions

Do you specialize in multi-day Indian wedding rituals?

+
Absolutely. From the vibrant Mehendi and Sangeet nights to the serene morning of the Juron (Assamese pre-wedding ritual), we manage the distinct timelines and cultural nuances of every ceremony with precision.

Can you source traditional Assamese handloom for decor?

+
Yes, we take pride in our roots. We collaborate with local artisans to incorporate authentic Muga and Eri silk drapes, Gamosa motifs, and bamboo-crafted elements into our high-end wedding designs.

Do you handle destination weddings outside of Assam?

+
While we are based in the heart of the Northeast, we travel globally. Whether it’s a palace wedding in Rajasthan or an intimate ceremony in the tea gardens of Upper Assam, our team handles all logistics.

What is the ideal timeline to begin planning?

+
For large-scale Indian weddings, we recommend 8 to 12 months. This allows us to secure the finest heritage venues and book the most sought-after traditional artists and vendors.
